# Amazon Optimization Tips

## Main Image Best Practices

### Image Requirements (Amazon Standard)
- **Dimensions**: 1600 x 1600 pixels or larger
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Background**: White background recommended (#FFFFFF)
- **Product Size**: Product should occupy 50-75% of image
- **Focus**: Product must be in focus and well-lit
- **Angle**: 3/4 front view preferred

### What Works on Amazon

✅ **DO THIS:**
- Pure white backgrounds (most professional)
- Studio lighting (professional appearance)
- Product centered in frame
- Sharp focus on entire product
- High contrast
- Multiple viewing angles in gallery
- Consistent lighting across image series
- Professional photography quality

❌ **AVOID THIS:**
- Colored backgrounds (unless approved)
- Lifestyle images as main image
- Text overlays on main image
- Watermarks or logos on main image
- Model hands or faces in main image
- Shadows or harsh lighting
- Blurry or out-of-focus areas
- Low-quality or compressed images

**Images 7-8 (Lifestyle):**

## Text Overlay Best Practices

### When to Use Text (Images 2-6)

✅ **Effective Text Usage:**
- Highlight key features
- Emphasize unique selling points
- Add trust signals (e.g., "Certified")
- Show quick benefits
- Guide customer attention
- Build emotional connection

### Text Design Rules

**Readability**
- Font size: 48-72pt for headlines
- Font size: 24-36pt for supporting text
- Sans-serif fonts (Montserrat, Poppins)
- High contrast (white on dark, dark on light)
- Bold weight for headlines
- Regular weight for body

**Positioning**
- Place in corners or edges
- Never cover critical product details
- Maintain 40px margin from image edges
- Use semi-transparent background if needed
- Center is last resort (only if product in corners)

**Content**
- Maximum 2 lines headline
- Maximum 2 lines supporting text
- Action verbs (Transform, Discover, Achieve)
- Specific metrics when possible
- Scannable format (not paragraphs)

**Example Good Text**
```
Premium Quality | Lifetime Warranty
Built to last with reinforced stitching
```

**Example Poor Text**
```
Our product is really great and has many
features that customers love and it's
the best choice for anyone who wants
quality and value.
```

## Color & Lighting Optimization

### White Background Optimization
- Use pure white (#FFFFFF) or near-white (#F8F8F8)
- Ensure background is perfectly uniform
- Use professional lighting to avoid shadows
- Slight drop shadow acceptable (very subtle)
- Maintains professional appearance

### Lighting for Product Showcase

**Key Light**: Main light source (45° angle from front)
**Fill Light**: Secondary light to reduce shadows
**Backlight**: Optional (creates depth and separation)
**Exposure**: Even across entire product
**Shadows**: Minimal or controlled

### Color Grading
- Accurate to actual product color
- Slightly enhanced (vibrant but not fake)
- Consistent with real-world appearance
- Warm white balance for inviting feel
- No extreme saturation

## Product Positioning Best Practices

### Angle Selection

**3/4 Front View** (Recommended)
- Shows most appealing perspective
- Reveals depth and dimension
- Highlights key features
- Natural viewing angle

**Straight-On View** (Alternative)
- Works for symmetrical products
- Good for flat design
- Shows scale clearly

**Angled/Side View** (Secondary)
- Shows depth
- Reveals thickness/dimension
- Good for second image

### Product Placement
- **Centered**: Traditional, professional
- **Off-center**: Can be more dynamic (use carefully)
- **Fills Frame**: 60-70% for main, 40-60% for others
- **Room to Breathe**: Don't crop too close

## Size Reference & Scale

### Showing Product Scale

**Effective Methods**
- Hand holding product
- Product next to common object (coin, ruler, cup)
- Product on standard surface
- Size comparison graphic
- Scale reference in lifestyle image

**Why It Matters**
- Customers can't touch or hold product
- Size is critical purchase factor
- Prevents negative reviews
- Reduces returns

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Technical Mistakes
❌ Image too small (less than 1600px)
❌ Wrong color space (CMYK instead of RGB)
❌ Compressed or pixelated images
❌ Watermarks or external URLs
❌ File format not PNG or JPEG
❌ Image rotated or distorted

### Content Mistakes
❌ Competitor products visible
❌ Misleading or false claims
❌ Model faces in main image
❌ Cluttered or confusing layout
❌ Inconsistent color grading
❌ Poor lighting or underexposed
❌ All images looking the same

### Design Mistakes
❌ Text too small to read
❌ Text overlaying critical product area
❌ Too much text on image
❌ Inconsistent fonts across images
❌ Distracting or clashing colors
❌ Unprofessional appearance

## Image Optimization Workflow

### Step 1: Prepare
- Gather reference images
- Define product positioning
- Ensure good lighting conditions
- Clean product (if physical photo)

### Step 2: Create
- Shoot/render at high quality
- Capture multiple angles
- Proper lighting and exposure
- Consistent color grading

### Step 3: Edit
- Crop to 1600x1600
- Color correct
- Adjust contrast/saturation
- Remove blemishes
- Add text if needed

### Step 4: Export
- Export as PNG or JPEG
- Verify 1600x1600 pixels
- Check RGB color space
- Keep file under 2MB
- 72 DPI for web

### Step 5: Verify
- Check file properties
- Preview at actual size
- Verify readability
- Check for artifacts
- Quality review pass

### Step 6: Upload
- Upload to Amazon
- Verify display correctly
- Check on mobile view
- Monitor for any issues

## Mobile Optimization

### Considerations for Mobile Viewers
- **Thumbnail View**: Images appear at ~300px
- **Full View**: Images display at device width
- **Text Readability**: Must be legible at small sizes
- **Contrast**: Critical for mobile viewing
- **Color Accuracy**: Mobile displays vary

### Mobile-Friendly Text
- Larger font sizes work better
- Shorter text (fewer words)
- High contrast colors
- Simple fonts
- Test on actual mobile device

## Performance & Upload

### File Size Optimization
- **JPEG**: Good for photographs, ~1-2MB
- **PNG**: Better for graphics with text, ~2-3MB
- **Amazon Limit**: 2MB per image suggested
- **Compression**: Minimal compression, maintain quality

### Upload Tips
- Upload in correct order (main first)
- Wait for each upload to complete
- Verify display correctly
- Allow 24-48 hours for full indexing
- Monitor first sales for feedback
