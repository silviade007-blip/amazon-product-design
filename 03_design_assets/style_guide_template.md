## Quality Standards

### Image Quality Requirements
- **Resolution**: 1600 x 1600 pixels (exact)
- **DPI**: 72 DPI (for web)
- **Color Space**: RGB (not CMYK)
- **File Format**: PNG or JPEG
- **File Size**: Under 2MB per image
- **Sharpness**: Crisp at full resolution

### Content Requirements
- **Focus**: Clear and sharp product/subject
- **Background**: Clean, professional, distraction-free
- **Lighting**: Professional quality, even exposure
- **Color Grading**: Consistent across image set
- **Watermarks**: Minimal or none (brand logo acceptable)
