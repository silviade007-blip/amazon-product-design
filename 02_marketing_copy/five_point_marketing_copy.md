## Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Recommended Tone
- [ ] Professional & Authoritative
- [ ] Friendly & Approachable
- [ ] Luxury & Exclusive
- [ ] Tech-Savvy & Modern
- [ ] Natural & Eco-Conscious
- [ ] Health-Focused & Scientific

### Writing Rules
✅ **DO:**
- Use action verbs (Transform, Discover, Achieve, Experience)
- Include specific numbers and metrics
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Use "you/your" language
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Make claims verifiable and honest

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use vague language ("good", "nice", "great")
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Use jargon your customers won't understand
- Oversell or exaggerate
- Use multiple exclamation marks
- Use ALL CAPS (except for emphasis)

---

## Final Copy Checklist

- [ ] All 5 selling points completed
- [ ] Each point focuses on a different benefit
- [ ] Headlines are 5-8 words
- [ ] Descriptions are 1-2 sentences max
- [ ] All claims are truthful and verifiable
- [ ] Copy is in US English
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Tone is consistent across all points
- [ ] Text is scannable (short paragraphs)
- [ ] Benefits are customer-focused
